- started working on terni lapilli
- made the basic move selection, including invalid move selection
    - maintained two states for the starting and ending squares of a move
- prevented player making a move to the same square
    - checked if the player's starting and ending square of a move are equal


============
April 19
============
- allowed player to actually be able to move pieces by updating the board
  with their old position as null and their new position as whatever game
  piece they are (X or O)

- restricts player to only moving pieces of their own type, by checking if
  the player clicks on a square of their own piece type (X or O)
    - X can only move X pieces, O can only move O pieces

- added checking to only allow moves to empty squares by checking if 
  a move will move into an occupied square

- added checking to only allow moves to adjacent squares by keeping a 
  list of valid moves and only allowing moves in this list

- added a message to inform the players of an invalid move by keeping a
  state that becomes true when an invalid move is played

- added feature to enforce players to vacate the center square on their turn
  unless they can win on their next move

- added custom messages to specify which type of illegal move was played
  0: there is no error in the move
  1: the player didnt select own piece
  2: the player moved to occupied or non-neighboring square
  3: the player violated vacancy rule by failing to vacate the center square or winning
     if they are occupying the center square
